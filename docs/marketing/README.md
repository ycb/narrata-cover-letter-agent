## ✨ Key Features of This Landing Page

### Strategic Positioning
- **Target Audience**: Mid-career Product Managers actively job searching
- **Core Value Prop**: "Fastest way to write high-fit cover letters from your real experience"
- **Differentiation**: Truth-based story library + real-time job matching vs. generic AI

### Content Structure
- **Hero**: Headline + subheadline + primary CTA + hero screenshot
- **Before/After**: Visual comparison showing problem → solution
- **6 Core Benefits**: Each benefit = headline + body copy + product screenshot
- **4-Step Workflow**: Upload → Paste JD → Get Draft → Improve & Finalize
- **Bottom CTA**: Reinforcement with testimonial placeholder

### Single CTA Strategy
- **One action**: "Get Started (Free)" throughout entire page
- **No pricing tiers**: No subscription plans, no multi-CTA confusion
- **Freemium entry**: "Takes 3 minutes. No credit card required."

To maintain focus and credibility:

❌ No testimonials (placeholder only for MVP)  
❌ No features not yet implemented (LinkedIn messages, resume optimization)  
❌ No positioning as "AI résumé builder" (cover letters only)  
❌ No technical jargon (LLMs, embeddings, RAG)  
❌ No competitor name-dropping (differentiate through features)

## 💬 Tone & Voice Reference

**Voice**: Clear, confident, product-forward  
**Speak to PMs as peers**: Not aspirational, not condescending  
**Emphasize structure + intelligence**: Not "AI magic"

### Good ✅
- "Narrata builds your strengths and stories into a reusable library"
- "See your fit in real time"
- "High-fit cover letters grounded in your actual work history"
- "You approve every word"

### Avoid ❌
- "Powered by cutting-edge LLMs"
- "Revolutionary AI technology"
- "10x better than ChatGPT"
- "Game-changing career tool"
